This page documents our styling reference

Currently, this pertains to the following:

  • All backgrounds make use of the (--background/--lighter) colour
  • All of our colours are centered around our 3 main colours (--primary, --secondary, --accent)
  • Furthermore, our shades are based around 4 colours (--lighter, --light, --dark, --darker)
  • The main font utilized is Helvetica.
  • Outside of lines, REM/EM gets almost always used over PX

This will be expanded upon down the line, once components get substantiated.
